About

The story behind the beats.

Vaynex is a boom-bap producer from Switzerland with one rule: no compromises. The sound is built on dusty drums, hand-picked samples and the raw, head-nodding energy that defined the golden era of hip-hop.

After a deliberate restart as a beatmaker — analog over algorithm, craft over shortcuts — Vaynex keeps releasing beats and collaborations for independent artists who want substance, not filler.

Open for collabs, custom beats and features. If you rap with intent, we'll get along.
